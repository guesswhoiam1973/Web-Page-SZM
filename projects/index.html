A formal project portfolio is currently in development. In the meantime, this page outlines the types of work SZM Architecture is focused on and the kind of project output clients can expect.

Residential

Homes & small residential work

Planning, design development, and drawing packages for homes, additions, and small residential projects with a focus on clarity, efficiency, and buildable output.

Commercial

Commercial & small business spaces

Spatial planning, concept design, and documentation for offices, service spaces, and small commercial buildings requiring a structured design process and coordinated drawings.

Mixed-Use

Small mixed-use projects

Early design studies and documentation support for compact mixed-use developments where layout, circulation, and future growth need to be considered from the start.

What a project may include

Typical project outputs.

Depending on scope, project work may include concept studies, design development, BIM-based drafting, approval drawings, and construction-oriented documentation.

Concept

Early-stage design

Concept layouts, site response, zoning logic, preliminary plans, elevations, and presentation views.

Development

Design refinement

Dimensioned plans, coordinated sections and elevations, and development of the selected design direction.

Documentation

Drawing production

Approval-ready and construction-oriented drawing sets developed through a clear, structured process.
