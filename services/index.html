01 / Process

Project workflow

Projects may be engaged at one stage only or carried through as a complete service, depending on scope, budget, and what information is already in place.

01

Consultation

Initial brief, site review, expectations, constraints, and scope definition.

02

Concept Design

Early planning, layout direction, massing, and general design intent.

03

Design Development

Refinement, coordination, dimensioning, and a more resolved technical direction.

04

Documentation

Drawing information developed for approval, coordination, and construction-oriented use.

05

Optional Services

Additional drafting and support services added only where they are actually needed.

02 / Packages

Service packages

The package structure allows projects to begin with design only, continue through development, or move toward a fuller drawing set.

Package A

Concept Design

Best suited for clients in early planning stages who need design direction, general layout thinking, and a stronger starting point before moving into technical development.

Package B

Design + Development

Best suited for clients who require a more coordinated, dimensioned, and resolved design before progressing toward construction planning.

Package C

Full Service

Best suited for clients who require a fuller, construction-ready drawing package with broader technical coverage and supporting information.
