01 / Overview

A design process built to move projects forward clearly.

SZM Architecture supports projects through a structured approach that starts with clear direction, develops through coordinated design thinking, and progresses toward practical drawing output.

02 / In sequence

From concept to documentation.

Projects are developed in stages so the scope stays clear and the information builds progressively rather than all at once.

Stage 01

Concept Design

Establishes the overall direction of the building — layout, spatial organization, design intent, and early visual development.

Stage 02

Design Development

Refines the selected design through more coordinated plans, sections, elevations, dimensions, and clearer technical intent.

Stage 03

Documentation

Produces the drawing information required for approval, coordination, and construction-oriented use, depending on scope.
