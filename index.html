03 / Process

How the process works

Each stage builds on approved work from the previous stage, allowing projects to stop early or continue through full documentation depending on scope.

01

Consultation

Brief review, site assessment, priorities, constraints, and project direction.

02

Concept Design

Layout, zoning, circulation, massing, and early facade direction.

03

Development

Refinement, dimensioned drawings, coordination, and design resolution.

04

Documentation

Construction-oriented drawings, schedules, notes, and coordinated outputs.

05

Optional Support

BOQ, take-offs, tender support, and project-specific add-on packages.

04 / Packages

Service packages

Packages are presented as a progressive service ladder, from concept direction to a complete drawing set.

Package A

Concept Design

Best suited for clients in early planning stages who need design direction before committing to detailed development.

  • Concept floor plans
  • Basic layouts and visuals
  • Preliminary elevations where applicable
Package B

Design + Development

For clients who require a coordinated and dimensioned design before technical construction planning begins.

  • Dimensioned plans
  • Developed elevations and sections
  • Refined and coordinated design set
Package C

Full Service

Best suited for clients who require a complete, construction-ready drawing package with full documentation.

  • Detailed plans, sections, elevations
  • Schedules, notes, and basic details
  • Coordinated PDF drawing set
