01 / Studio

A clear process from concept to documentation.

SZM Architecture approaches projects through a structured design process that balances clarity, coordination, and practical project support. The aim is not just to produce drawings, but to move projects forward in an intentional way.

02 / What the work covers

Design, development, and buildable output.

Work typically begins with concept design, develops through coordinated refinement, and progresses toward documentation suitable for approval and construction-oriented use, depending on project scope.

03 / Project types

Residential, commercial, and small mixed-use projects.

The studio supports homes, additions, small business spaces, and compact mixed-use work where good planning, strong drawing coordination, and a clear workflow matter from the beginning.
