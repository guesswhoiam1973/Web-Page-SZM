About

A measured studio approach grounded in clarity and coordination.

SZM Architecture provides architectural design and BIM-based documentation services for residential, commercial, and small mixed-use projects, with a focus on structured progression and clearly communicated outputs.

Studio

What the practice offers

The studio works across concept development, design refinement, documentation, and project support. The emphasis is on making the process legible to clients before moving into more technical stages.

Approach

How projects are handled

Each project is developed in defined stages so that decisions are made at the right level of detail. This helps reduce confusion, improve coordination, and support more controlled project development.

01 / Method

A process-led way of working.

Rather than compressing everything into one step, projects are broken into stages so the design can be understood, reviewed, refined, and documented with greater control.

Clarity

Clear progression

Each stage is intended to make the next one easier to understand, so project decisions are not rushed or buried under technical information too early.

Coordination

Structured development

Design thinking, drafting, and technical output are developed in a way that supports coordination rather than fragmented production.

Output

Buildable information

The end goal is not only a resolved design direction, but drawing output that is practical, usable, and aligned with project scope.

02 / Focus

Projects scaled to real requirements.

The practice is oriented toward residential, commercial, and small mixed-use work where structured design, documentation, and measured technical progression are especially valuable.

Scope

Flexible engagement

Some projects begin at consultation or concept stage, while others continue through fuller design development and documentation. The service path is shaped around what the project actually needs.

Intent

Measured, not overloaded

The approach avoids unnecessary complexity at the beginning of a project and instead builds information progressively, so decisions remain clearer for both client and project team.

03 / Next step

Begin with a consultation or project enquiry.

If you are considering a project and want to determine the right starting point, book a consultation or send an enquiry with the basic scope and location.
