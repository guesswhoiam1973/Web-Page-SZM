About

A measured studio approach grounded in clarity and coordination.

SZM Architecture provides architectural design and BIM-based documentation services for residential, commercial, and small mixed-use projects, with a focus on structured progression and clearly communicated outputs.

Studio

What the practice offers

The studio works across concept development, design refinement, documentation, and project support. The emphasis is on making the process legible to clients before moving into more technical stages.

Approach

How projects are handled

Each project is developed in defined stages so that decisions are made at the right level of detail. This helps reduce confusion, improve coordination, and support more controlled project development.
